What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your user account and profile information
  • Your saved delivery addresses
  • Your cart items and preferences
  • Your notification preferences
  • Your app usage data and analytics

What Data May Be Retained

For legal and business purposes, we may retain certain information:

  • Transaction Records: Order history and payment records may be retained for accounting and tax purposes (typically 7 years as required by law)
  • Legal Compliance: Information required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements
  • Anonymized Data: Aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you may be retained for analytics purposes
Note: If you have active orders or pending transactions, please wait until these are completed before requesting account deletion. We cannot delete accounts with active orders.
